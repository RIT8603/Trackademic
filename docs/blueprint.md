# **App Name**: Campus Sentinel

## Core Features:

- Automated Attendance: Capture real-time video from classroom cameras and use face recognition (leveraging a tool) to mark attendance automatically.
- Academic Planner: Suggest activities during free periods, aligning with studentsâ€™ strengths, interests, and career goals, as detected and analyzed by our tool.
- Real-time Dashboards: Provide real-time attendance transparency on student & teacher dashboards.
- Faculty Analytics: Present student engagement trends and daily attendance reports via analytics dashboards.
- Idle-Time Activity Suggestions: Integrate a chatbot that suggests academic tasks/projects during idle time, adapting the suggestions based on real-time information and analytics from other sources as decided by the tool.
- Security Alerts: Detect unauthorized activities (violence, fights) via camera and alert relevant personnel.
- Absenteeism Alerts: Send alerts to students & parents regarding absenteeism.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep Blue (#3F51B5) to convey trust and authority, referencing the 'Smart Automation in Education' theme, while avoiding cliches. 
- Background color: Light Gray-Blue (#E8EAF6), visibly desaturated relative to the primary and creating a dark scheme.
- Accent color: Purple (#7E57C2) providing contrast and drawing attention to key elements.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif font known for its modern, neutral, and objective appearance; it will serve effectively for both headlines and body text.
- Use simple, clear icons to represent attendance status, activity types, and alert categories.
- Implement a clean and intuitive layout that balances information density with ease of use, prioritizing key metrics.
- Employ subtle animations and transitions to provide feedback and guide users through the interface without being distracting.
